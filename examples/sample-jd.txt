Why now

Institutional rental housing is entering a new era. Legacy CRMs, point
solutions, spreadsheets and fragmented workflows are no longer fit for
purpose. Lette is building an AI-native, agentic back office that automates
property operations end to end. Think Rippling plus Anthropic for rental
portfolios.

We are shipping daily and already powering live operations. With ambitious
goals for 2026 and beyond, this is your chance to shape the product, team
and company from the start.

What you will be building

You will work closely with the founders and early customers to design,
build, and scale the core platform. This is not a narrow IC role. You will
influence architecture, product direction, and how we work as a team.

You will:

- Work across the stack using TypeScript, React, Node.js, Prisma and
  PostgreSQL
- Help deliver intelligent, agentic operations across leasing, rent
  reconciliation, maintenance, compliance, and finance
- Integrate with core systems, fintech providers, and industry APIs
- Leverage our distilled LLMs and predictive models through tools like
  SageMaker, Bedrock, Arcade, and other AI-native infrastructure to power
  smarter workflows
- Own features end to end with full autonomy from design to deployment

We do not expect you to be familiar with all the tools we use. What
matters is your curiosity and ability to learn fast.

We move fast, cut scope ruthlessly, and ship value daily via feature
flags. You will be instrumental in shaping both the product and
engineering culture from day one.

What you'll bring

- 8+ years in fast-paced engineering environments, ideally at a startup
  or scale-up
- Strong experience with TypeScript/JavaScript, React (or similar), and
  Node.js
- Exposure to infrastructure and backend (we use Prisma + PostgreSQL,
  hosted on AWS Fargate via CDK)
- Product-mindedness: you care about what you build and why it matters
- AI-native thinking: excited about what AI can do and how to use it
  practically (e.g. prompt chaining, function calling, semantic search)
- High agency: you find problems, fix them, and communicate clearly
- Generalist mindset: happy to jump between frontend, backend, infra,
  and debugging
- Strong collaboration: async and remote-first experience via Slack,
  Notion, Linear, GitHub

Nice to have:

- Experience with scheduling systems, workflows, or proptech
- Familiarity with Anthropic, Pinecone, or vector databases
- Experience building internal tools or agentic flows using LangChain,
  LlamaIndex, or similar

What you will get

- Founding-level equity
- Highly competitive salary depending on location and experience
- Work from anywhere
- 35 days paid leave (incl. Bank Holidays)
- Quarterly team off-sites to connect, build and reset
